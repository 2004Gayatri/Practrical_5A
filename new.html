Welcome to MCOE

MCOE is spread over 15.6 acres with greenery, accredited four times by NBA, and graded 'A+' by NAAC. It boasts a state-of-the-art IT center, a library with over 66,735 books, 129 print journals, and 275 e-journals. The institute offers 100% placement assistance and provides foreign language training (Japanese, German) and more.

Vision

To create opportunities for rural students to become skilled engineers and technocrats through excellence in engineering education.

Mission

Our mission is to create self-disciplined, physically fit, mentally robust, and morally strong engineers capable of meeting the challenges of advancing technology for the benefit of mankind and nature.
